# The three-minute walkthrough · word for word

A screen recording for WhatsApp, phone held upright, the back office
open at www.aumosaic.com/admin. Read it as written; the words fit
under three minutes at a calm pace. Do each bracketed action as you
say the line above it.

Before recording: sign in, pick any order with a line or two on it,
and know one piece you can edit without harm. Quiet room, one take
is enough, small stumbles are human and fine.

---

**[Open on the glance, the five numbers]**

Nonso, this is your back office. This first screen is the morning
glance. Five numbers: pieces in your catalogue, stock warnings, open
orders, money outstanding, and new enquiries. Three seconds, and you
know if the house is okay.

**[Tap the gold New order button]**

A customer wants to buy. One gold button, New order.

**[Pick a customer, save, land on the order]**

Pick who is buying, save, and the order opens its own page.

**[Tap into Add a line. Point at List price, then Given price]**

Here is the part built for you. Every line has two prices. List is
the real price. Given is what you actually said after the talking.
The gap between them is your discount, and now it is a number you
can see, not a feeling. That number is on the insights page every
morning.

**[Add the line, then scroll to Record a payment]**

When money comes in, record it here. Amount, how it came, done.
The balance updates itself. You never carry it in your head again.

**[Open Who owes what from the tab bar]**

This room is the one you asked for first. Everyone owing the house,
the longest forgotten debt on top. See this gold button? It writes
the reminder message for you. One tap, it opens in WhatsApp, you
just press send. Nobody is forgotten on paper again.

**[Open the stockroom, tap one piece]**

The stockroom. Every piece you sell, tiles and pool materials both.
Open a piece and everything about it is one form: the words on the
site, the colours, how many are in stock, and when to warn you.
Change the number, save, done. If stock runs low, the glance tells
you by itself.

**[Scroll to the photographs on the piece record]**

Photos go here, straight from your phone. A night shot and a day
shot. Put one up and the website wears it the same minute.

**[Point at the switch, Show on the site]**

And this switch decides if customers can see the piece at all. On,
it is in your window. Off, it waits in the book.

**[Back to the glance]**

That is the whole office. It lives on your phone like an app, it
works when the network is weak, and nothing you write in it is ever
lost. Start with one real order today, and message me if anything
feels off. Welcome to the back office.
