How to use it

WhatsApp does the talking and the paying. The book keeps the count for you, from the first tap to settled.

Step three, up close · The number is the key

Turn a chat into a customer

Copy once, paste once.

  1. In WhatsApp, tap the customer's name at the top to open their info.
  2. Long-press their number and tap Copy.
  3. In AU Mosaic, open Customers and tap New customer.
  4. Tap Paste. The number drops in, cleaned. Add their name.
  5. Save. You are one tap from their first order.

Works the same on Android and iPhone. Copy and paste is the whole trick.

Every room, one line each

The glance

  • The day at once: what is owed, what is low, what just came in.

Customers

  • Everyone you have saved.
  • Fresh from the window: the taps off your website.
  • Open one for their orders, balance, and history.
  • Add a customer: name, phone, area, a note.
  • Paste a WhatsApp number to add someone in seconds.
  • Tap to open their WhatsApp.
  • Set a to-do, and tick it off when done.

Orders

  • Every order and where it sits: enquiry, quoted, deposit, delivered, settled.
  • Start one by choosing the customer.
  • Add lines: a tile or free text, quantity, your price and the price you gave.
  • Move it one step along with a tap.
  • Add a return without erasing anything.
  • Send the quote or receipt on WhatsApp, already written.
  • Open the invoice.

Money

  • Record a payment: the amount, and how it came, transfer or cash.
  • A refund is just a minus.
  • The balance works itself out: billed, less paid.

Who owes you

  • Everyone who still owes, oldest first.
  • Send a gentle reminder on WhatsApp in one tap.
  • Download the list as a spreadsheet.

Deliveries

  • Book a delivery with an address and a day.
  • Mark it out, then delivered.

Invoices

  • Open a clean invoice for any order.
  • Print it, to paper or to PDF.

Tiles and ranges

  • See and edit every piece and range you sell.
  • Set stock counts and a warn-me level.
  • The book tells you when a piece runs low.

Photos

  • See every photo.
  • Approve or archive.
  • Choose which ones the website shows.

Insights

  • What is selling, and how the month is going.

Settings

  • Change your own facts: WhatsApp number, hours, address.
  • Give staff their own keys, and turn on alerts.
  • History: every change in plain sentences, nothing lost.

It works with no signal, and it speaks your language, never computer talk.
