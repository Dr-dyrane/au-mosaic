# Your back office, in plain words

WhatsApp does the talking and the paying, the way it always has. The book just
remembers everything around it: who, what, how much, owed, delivered. Nothing
here changes how you sell. It only keeps the count for you.

## Turn a WhatsApp chat into a customer

The number is the key, and copy and paste is the whole trick. It works the same on Android and iPhone.

1. In WhatsApp, tap the customer's name at the top to open their info.
2. Long-press their number and tap Copy.
3. In AU Mosaic, open Customers and tap New customer.
4. Tap Paste. The number drops into the box, cleaned. Add their name.
5. Save. From their record you are one tap from their first order and their WhatsApp.

That is it. Nothing to install, nothing to share.

## Everything you can do, one line each

The glance

- See the day at once: what is owed, what is low, what just came in.

Customers

- See everyone you have saved.
- Read "fresh from the window": the taps that came off your website.
- Open a customer to see their orders, their balance, their history.
- Add a new customer: name, phone, area, a note.
- Tap to open their WhatsApp.
- Set a to-do against them, like a showroom visit or send sample pictures, and tick it off when done.

Orders

- See every order and where it sits: Enquiry, Quoted, Deposit paid, Delivered, Settled.
- Start an order by choosing the customer.
- Add lines: a tile or free text, the quantity, your normal price and the price you gave.
- Move the order one step along with a single tap.
- Add a return without erasing anything.
- Send the quote or the receipt to the customer on WhatsApp, already written.
- Open the invoice.

Money

- Record a payment: the amount, and how it came, transfer or cash. A refund is just a minus.
- The balance works itself out: what was billed, less what was paid.

Who owes you

- See everyone who still owes, the oldest first.
- Send a gentle reminder on WhatsApp in one tap.
- Download the list as a spreadsheet.

Deliveries

- Book a delivery with an address and a day.
- Mark it out, then delivered.

Invoices

- Open a clean invoice for any order and print it, to paper or to PDF.

Your tiles and ranges

- See and edit every piece and range you sell.
- Set your stock counts and a "warn me" level, and the book tells you when a piece runs low.

Photos

- See every photo, approve or archive, and choose which ones the website shows.

Insights

- See what is selling and how the month is going.

Settings

- Change your own facts: the WhatsApp number, the hours, the address, no developer needed.
- Give staff their own keys, and turn on alerts.
- History: read every change in plain sentences. Nothing is ever truly lost.

And everywhere: it works with no signal, and it speaks your language, never computer talk.
